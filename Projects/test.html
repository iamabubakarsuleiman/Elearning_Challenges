Fried Rice

Ingredients
Rice
Vegetables (carrots, peas, bell peppers)
Soy Sauce
Chicken or shrimp
Garlic and onions

Steps

1. Cook the rice and let it cool.

2. Sauté garlic and onions in oil.

3. Add vegetables and cook until tender.

4. Add rice, soy sauce, and protein, stir-frying everything together.

5. Serve hot and enjoy!

Rice and Stew

Ingredients
Rice
Tomatoes
Onions
Bell peppers
Meat (chicken, beef)

Steps

1. Cook rice and set aside.

2. Blend tomatoes, onions, and peppers to make the stew base.

3. Sauté onions, then add the blended mix and simmer.

4. Add cooked meat and let everything cook together.

5. Serve with rice.

Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients
Spaghetti
Ground beef
Tomato sauce
Onions and garlic
Herbs (basil, oregano)

Steps

1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.

2. Sauté onions and garlic, then add ground beef and cook until browned.

3. Add tomato sauce and herbs, simmer until thickened.

4. Serve the sauce over spaghetti.
